Sri Sri Harichand Thakur

Sri Sri Harichand Thakur was born on 11 march 1811 (Bangla 29 Falgun, 1218 Bagaand, wednesday Madhukrishna trayodashi tithi) in a Namashudra family in the Safaldanga village of Gopalganj District which is now a part of Bangladesh. He was the son of Jashomanta Thakur and Annapurna Devi. Later he and his family relocated to Odakandi of the same village.

He is the Avatar of Mahavishnu in Kaliyuga, he is called PurnaBramha (God of all Gods). According to him, the best Yogi is the person who can express his devotion to God by staying in familty and handles all responsibilities. He is the founder of a sect of Hindus called Matua. He left his body on 5 March 1878 (23 Falgun 1284 Bagaand wednesday) in Odakandi, Faridpur District.

Sri Sri Guruchand Thakur

Sri Sri Guruchand Thakur was born on 11 march 1847 (Bangla 15 Falgun, 1253 Bagaand, friday) in Odakandi of Gopalganj District. He was the son of Sri Sri Harichand Thakur and Shanti Mata. He was a great leader who worked for uprising of poor and untouchable people in the indian society.

He is considered the Avatar of Lord Shiva. Under his strong leadership, the Matua section achieved a major organizational push and started the Namasudra protest movement. He understood the importance of education through the experience of his own life. He realized that only education can uplift the Dalit society, can bring the desired equality between different castes. For that he established many schools for the upliftment of the Dalit community. He left his body in Odakandi, 1937.

About Harichand Sevasangh Pakhanjore

Harichand Sevasangh Pakhanjore was founded on december 1969 by Aacharya Mahananda Haldar. During his first visit in Paralkot area he formed Harichand sevasangh society and started a branch of Matua Sevasangh in Pakhanjore Subdistrict. He formed the structure of the society, defined various position and filled them with local people of Paralkot. Later on 1972 the administration of Pakhanjore provided an official Land to the Matua community for the Aasram, Schoool creation and other activities.

The Aasram

The primary purpose of Harichand Sevasangh is to provide education and help needy people, the Aasram is located in Pakhanjore subdistrict of Kanker District Chhattisgarh and 235 KM from capital Raipur. It spread over an area of 5 acre consisting of Nat Mandir, Kamona Sagar, one School, rest house and some rooms for the living of Pujari, storage and other stuff. Annual ceremony called MahaBarooni is take place every year on Madhukrishna trayodashi. A huge Utsava where thousands of devotees, saints and matua people gather for the celebration of birth anniversary of sri sri Harichand Thakur.
