Sri Sri Harichand Thakur

Sri Sri Harichand Thakur was born in 1811 (madhukrishna tryodashi) in a Namashudra family in the Safaldanga village of Gopalganj District which is now a part of Bangladesh. He was the son of Jashomanta Biswas and Annapurna Devi.

He is the Avatar of Mahavishnu in Kaliyuga, he is called PurnaBramha (God of all Gods). According to him, the best Yogi is the person who can express his devotion to God by staying in familty and handles all responsibilities. He is the founder of a sect of Hindus called Matua. He left his body on 1878 in Faridpur District.
